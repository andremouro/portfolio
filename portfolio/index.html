Relevant production

I'm a former biologist with phD in Ecology. My researches focused on plant-soil feedbacks and ecosystem restoration. During this time, I have discovered my passion for data, developing my skills in designing and implementing experimental studies, and data analysis and visualization using R and Python.

Here are some of the papers I have published during my days of researcher.

Microbial community role in restoration

In this paper I investigated the influence of microbial community in the outcome of restoration efforts in Brazillian Cerrado.

Plant-soil feedback regulate seedlings growth

This paper investigates how litter deposition influences soil microbial community, and its consequence to seedlings growth.

Corn and bacteria communication

This paper studies the mechanisms of 'communication' between corn seedlings and plant-growth promoting bacteria.

Termites effects on wood decomposition worldwide

I contributed to this group of researchers on the investigation of the influences of termites in wood decay rate in the globe.

Restoration techniques

In this paper we evaluated how restoration techniques successfully recover the functional composition of Brazilian Cerrado savanna.

Method improvement

I contributed to the mathematical modelling of this paper. In this work, Cinthia aimed to improve the methods of fine root collection during field work.

Soil inoculation effect on restoration

In this paper we evaluated how soil inoculum from different ecosystems (native or invaded) influences the outcome of restoration in Brazilian Cerrado.

Brazilian Cerrado Review

In this paper we made a review on the ecosystem dynamics in Cerrado and its relation with climatic and geomorphological conditions and fire.
